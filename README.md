# Knowledge Hubs 

The Global AI Liability Consortium develops AI liability through a growing network of specialized Knowledge Hubs.

Each Knowledge Hub serves as a dedicated platform for advancing practical knowledge within a specific domain of AI liability through research, reference libraries, professional resources, assessment tools, publications, and institutional collaboration.

Together, these Knowledge Hubs build the world's enduring knowledge infrastructure for AI liability.

> **Liability is where AI disciplines converge.**

---

# Purpose

Knowledge Hubs translate specialized expertise into practical knowledge for organizations, professionals, researchers, policymakers, and the public.

Each Knowledge Hub develops enduring resources that strengthen understanding, improve professional practice, and support responsible AI accountability.

While GAILC Departments organize expertise, Knowledge Hubs develop and share that expertise with the world.

---

# What Every Knowledge Hub Develops

Each Knowledge Hub may develop:

- Reference Libraries
- Research
- Professional Guidance
- Assessment Frameworks
- Checklists
- Templates
- Publications
- Educational Resources
- Case Studies
- Regulatory Analysis
- Best Practices
- Institutional Collaboration

The specific resources developed within each Knowledge Hub reflect the needs of its discipline.
